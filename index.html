Input Features

Three signals, all free, all automated

sst_z

Sea Surface Temperature

Z-score anomaly for Peru coastal box. Warmer surface = higher risk. Captures El Niño heating.

NOAA OISST → ERDDAP
chl_z

Chlorophyll-a

Z-score from Copernicus NRT with coastal mask. Lower chlorophyll = weaker upwelling = higher risk.

Copernicus Marine → OPeNDAP
nino12_t1

Niño 1+2 Index

Monthly eastern equatorial Pacific anomaly, lagged one month. The large-scale climate driver.

NOAA CPC → CSV
