## Timing

Best time to reach out: **immediately after a correct prediction.** If the 2026 S1 season shows stress consistent with your MODERATE forecast, that's your proof point. "My model said this would happen, and it did" is the strongest possible pitch.

Second best: when ENFEN or IMARPE makes a major announcement. You can reference their statement and show your model saw it coming.

Worst time: when everything is normal and the fishery is operating smoothly. Nobody cares about risk models when there's no risk.

**Current window is excellent** — ENFEN just confirmed El Niño Costero, your model shows MODERATE trending toward ELEVATED, and the season opens in ~6 weeks.
